### **Alternative OCR Solutions**

1. **Google Drive OCR**:

   - Right-click → "Open with Google Docs"
   - Google automatically runs OCR
   - Download as text document

2. **SmallPDF OCR**:
   - Visit: https://smallpdf.com/ocr-pdf
   - Upload your PDF
   - Download OCR-processed PDF

3. **ILovePDF OCR**:
   - Visit: https://www.ilovepdf.com/ocr-pdf
   - Upload and process
   - Download text-based PDF

#### **Desktop OCR Applications**
1. **Adobe Acrobat Pro**:
   - Open PDF → Tools → Enhance Scans
   - Run OCR processing
   - Save as searchable PDF

2. **ABBYY FineReader**:
   - Professional OCR software
   - High accuracy for cookbooks
   - Batch processing capabilities
